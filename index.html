UX PROCESS

USER RESEARCH | IA | WIREFRAME | PROTOTYPES

I start a project by defining and scoping the problem at hand. Then, I figure out the goal of the final product. After conducting user research, interviews and surveys, I begin to devise strategic solutions in the form of sitemaps, wireframes and prototypes. I test the usability of my design and iterate upon them to ensure an excellent user experience.

UI PROCESS

UI ELEMENTS | INTERACTIONS | BRANDING

I find the right brand language that forms the unique UI elements for each product. I make creative design decisions in order to best translate any given UX wireframe into a smooth and intuitive interaction. I often communicate my design through animated and interactive prototypes. Then, create the necessary files to develop the product.
