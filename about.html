Why I Love UX/UI Design

Design allows people to decipher information in a beautiful, easily comprehensible way. As they say, “knowledge is power.” So, when we give people an opportunity to comprehend information more easily, we give them an opportunity to grow and completely change their future. That’s what excites me about design!

More specifically, design has the potential to change someone’s everyday life for the better. When someone is navigating a real estate website and searching for their first home, their experience of buying that home could be completely tainted due to a bad user experience. However, if they are able to navigate the page with ease, they are given the space to dream, cherish that moment, and get enthusiastic about the exciting decision they are about to make. I want to be a part of making someone’s everyday life better by providing great every day experiences.

Secondly, design provided me an opportunity to learn about something I was passionate about, change careers, and dream again. I would love to see people have the same opportunities I’ve had by mentoring people as they change careers and help them do something they find deeply fulfilling.

The Path that Led to Design

I was introduced to design through a college art course and found true joy in creating and learning how to use structure along with creativity to make something beautiful. I ended up going down a different career path, but found myself always being drawn back to design and how exciting it was to create, innovate, and problem solve through design.

Even though my original career path wasn’t as fulfilling as I thought it would be, the time I spent there wasn’t wasted. I learned the immense value of receiving feedback, developing as a leader, and how to manage numerous projects at once. I believe these three key areas of growth have had and will continue to have an invaluable impact on me as I continue to learn how to become a better UX/UI Designer.
